\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Internal representation of temperature values. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The internal fixed point format has 9 bits (512 steps) per degree. The range is -\/16 to 112C, an offset of -\/48C
\item The communication over serial is in C or F and it is always converted to the internal fixed point format in C.
\item The interface to the Raspberry Pi uses decimal notation, like 21.\+3.
\item Depending on the E\+E\+P\+R\+OM setting cc.\+temp\+Format, this will be interpreted as Celsius or Fahrenheit
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{group__temps_autotoc_md0}{}\doxysubsubsection{Conversion}\label{group__temps_autotoc_md0}
\hypertarget{group__temps_autotoc_md1}{}\doxyparagraph{From C to fixed point temp}\label{group__temps_autotoc_md1}
\begin{eqnarray*} T &=& (C-48) \cdot 512 \\ &=& (C-48)<<9 \\ &=& (C<<9) - (48<<9) \\ &=& (C<<9) - 24576 \\ \end{eqnarray*}\hypertarget{group__temps_autotoc_md2}{}\doxyparagraph{From F to fixed point temp}\label{group__temps_autotoc_md2}
\begin{eqnarray*} T &=& ((F-32)\frac{9}{5}-48) \cdot 512 \\ &=& (F\frac{9}{5})<<9 - 33678 \end{eqnarray*}\hypertarget{group__temps_autotoc_md3}{}\doxyparagraph{From fixed point temp to C}\label{group__temps_autotoc_md3}
\begin{eqnarray*} C &=& \frac{T}{512} + 48 \\ &=& \frac{T+24576}{512} \\ &=& (T+24576)>>9 \end{eqnarray*}\hypertarget{group__temps_autotoc_md4}{}\doxyparagraph{From fixed point temp to F}\label{group__temps_autotoc_md4}
\begin{eqnarray*} F &=& (\frac{T}{512} + 48) \cdot \frac{9}{5} + 32 \\ &=& (T+24576) \cdot \frac{9}{5} \cdot 512 + 32 \\ &=& (T+33678) \cdot \frac{9}{5} \cdot 512 \end{eqnarray*}
